# Scoring Criteria: Multi-Dimensional Assessment Framework

## Evaluation Dimensions

### 1. Accuracy (0-100)
- **Correctness**: Factual accuracy of answers
- **Precision**: Specificity and detail level
- **Completeness**: Coverage of required elements
- **Conceptual Understanding**: Depth beyond memorization

### 2. Mastery Thresholds
- **80%+**: Ready to advance (mastery achieved)
- **60-79%**: Developing (targeted practice needed)
- **40-59%**: Struggling (intervention required)
- **<40%**: Foundational gaps (prerequisite review needed)

### 3. Trend Analysis
- **Improving**: Consistent upward trajectory (celebrate and maintain)
- **Plateauing**: Stagnant performance (change strategy)
- **Declining**: Downward trend (diagnose root cause)
- **Volatile**: Inconsistent results (address stability)

### 4. Pattern Recognition
- **Strengths**: Consistently correct topic areas
- **Weaknesses**: Recurring error patterns
- **Blind Spots**: Unrecognized knowledge gaps
- **Misconceptions**: Systematic incorrect beliefs

## Recommendation Framework
- Strength-based: Leverage what works
- Gap-targeted: Address specific weaknesses
- Strategy-adaptive: Adjust study methods based on data
- Motivation-aware: Balance challenge and achievability

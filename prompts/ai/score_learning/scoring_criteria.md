## Scoring Dimensions (0-100 scale)

**Accuracy (40%)**: Correctness of core concepts and facts
- 90-100: Fully correct, no errors
- 70-89: Minor errors, core understanding intact
- 50-69: Partial understanding, significant gaps
- 30-49: Major misconceptions
- 0-29: Fundamentally incorrect

**Completeness (30%)**: Coverage of required elements
- 90-100: All key points addressed
- 70-89: Most points covered
- 50-69: Partial coverage
- 0-49: Incomplete

**Depth (20%)**: Understanding beyond surface level
- 90-100: Deep insight, connections made
- 70-89: Good understanding
- 50-69: Surface-level grasp
- 0-49: Superficial

**Clarity (10%)**: Expression quality
- 90-100: Clear, well-organized
- 70-89: Mostly clear
- 50-69: Some confusion
- 0-49: Unclear
